Insights:
 1. Customers with month-to-month contracts churn more
 2. High monthly charges increase churn probability
 3. Customers with low tenure are more likely to churn
 4. Lack of additional services leads to higher churn




 Recommendations:
 1. Offer discounts for long-term contracts
 2. Provide loyalty rewards for long-tenure customers
 3. Reduce pricing for high-charge customers
 4. Improve customer service to reduce complaints
